The Premise

Not a planning retreat. A diagnostic.

Most strategic planning processes start from the assumption that the organisation knows what conditions it is operating in. Strategic Fitness begins one step earlier — by testing whether that assumption is justified.

It is a structured assessment of how well your current strategy, capabilities, and decision-making processes are aligned to the actual conditions facing your organisation — not the conditions that existed when the strategy was written.

The gap between those two things is almost always larger than leadership teams expect. Strategic Fitness makes that gap visible — and actionable.

“The most dangerous place any organisation can be is confidently executing a strategy that is no longer aligned to reality.”

Larry Quick · Co-founder, Resilient Futures

Strategic Fitness is the entry point to working with Resilient Futures. It generates an honest picture of strategic health in a single session — and gives leadership teams a clear basis for deciding what to do next.

The Diagnostic

Five dimensions. One honest assessment.

The Strategic Fitness diagnostic maps your organisation's strategic position across the five standards of Strategy in Action. Each dimension surfaces a different category of strategic risk — and a different category of strategic opportunity.

01
Conditions Clarity

Does your leadership team share a common, evidence-based understanding of the forces actually driving change in your operating environment?

Surfaces: blind spots, interpretation gaps, misaligned assumptions about what's changing and why.

02
Value Alignment

Is the value your organisation delivers still the value your stakeholders actually need — given the conditions it now faces?

Surfaces: silent relevance erosion, misaligned value propositions, unrecognised stakeholder drift.

03
Strategic Risk Exposure

Where is your organisation exposed to strategic risks that your current risk register cannot see — and which opportunities are being missed?

Surfaces: underweighted risks, overlooked opportunities, interdependencies your governance process is missing.

04
Capability Readiness

Are you building the capabilities conditions now require — or investing in the capabilities that mattered in conditions that no longer exist?

Surfaces: capability gaps widening silently, over-investment in legacy strengths, build vs. buy misjudgements.

05
Strategic Velocity

Can your organisation translate strategic clarity into consequential action quickly enough to remain relevant as conditions continue to shift?

Surfaces: decision lag, execution bottlenecks, strategy disconnected from operational pace.

One session. A complete strategic read.

Five dimensions mapped. Clear findings. Immediate action pathways. A basis for the Board and Executive team to decide what comes next — with shared confidence.

The Session

A structured day that generates real insight.

Strategic Fitness is available as a half-day (4 hours) or full-day (7 hours) format, depending on the depth of diagnostic required and the questions your organisation most needs answered. Both formats follow the same structured approach — the full-day allows for deeper interrogation of findings and extended action planning.

Phase 1
Pre-work & context
Conditions briefing

Before the session, Resilient Futures conducts a structured assessment of your operating environment — the forces driving change, their trajectory, and their implications for your specific organisation. This briefing is presented to the leadership team to establish a shared, evidence-based starting point.

Phase 2
Structured assessment
Five-dimension diagnostic

The leadership team works through the five diagnostic dimensions in structured, facilitated sessions. Each dimension uses a combination of pre-circulated diagnostics, live discussion, and scenario stress-testing to surface the real strategic position — not the one that looks best in a board paper.

Phase 3
Synthesis & findings
Strategic fitness read

Resilient Futures synthesises the diagnostic findings into a clear strategic fitness assessment — rating each dimension and identifying the specific areas requiring the most urgent attention. Findings are presented back to the full group for confirmation and challenge.

Phase 4
Action & next steps
Immediate action pathways

The session closes with concrete, prioritised action pathways — specific to your organisation's findings. These are not generic recommendations. They are the steps this organisation needs to take next, grounded in what the diagnostic has revealed.

Format Options
Half Day
Approx. 4 hours

Covers all five diagnostic dimensions with focused interrogation. Best suited for organisations seeking a rapid, high-level read of strategic fitness, or as a precursor to a fuller SiA engagement.

Full Day

Audience

Location

On-site at your organisation or at a facilitation venue. Available across Australia.

The Output

What you leave with.

Strategic Fitness is designed to generate actionable intelligence, not just insight. Every session concludes with a clear set of outputs that give the leadership team something to act on immediately.

Strategic Fitness Assessment

A structured, written read of your organisation's strategic fitness across all five dimensions — where you are strong, where you are exposed, and what the conditions are demanding that your current strategy is not yet addressing.

Priority Strategic Questions

The three to five strategic questions your Board and Executive team should be wrestling with right now — grounded in the diagnostic findings, not derived from a generic framework. These questions become the basis for your next strategic conversation.

Immediate Action Pathways

Specific, prioritised actions your organisation can take within 30 to 90 days to close the most critical gaps identified in the diagnostic. Not broad recommendations — concrete next steps, assigned to the right level of the organisation.

A Clear View of What Comes Next

A shared leadership-team view of whether a deeper SiA engagement is warranted — and if so, what the most important strategic work is. Strategic Fitness creates the basis for confident, aligned decision-making about where to invest your strategic energy next.

Who It Is For

Built for organisations operating in genuine complexity.

We work with Australian Boards and Executives whose organisations carry a stakeholder map as complicated as the environment around them — and where getting strategy right is genuinely non-negotiable.

Boards & CEOs

Strategic Fitness gives a Board and CEO a shared, independently-generated view of the organisation's strategic health — one that is not filtered through the management team's optimism or the prior year's assumptions.

Good fit when

Strategic confidence is low, governance conversations feel circular, or conditions have shifted materially and the Board needs an independent read.

Senior Leadership Teams

When the Executive team needs to stress-test their strategic assumptions, identify where capability investment should go, and surface the strategic questions they may be avoiding, Strategic Fitness provides a structured process to do exactly that.

Good fit when

The team is executing well but uncertain about whether they are executing the right strategy, or when there is misalignment in the room about where to focus.

Organisations at Inflection Points

Sector disruption, leadership transition, funding model change, regulatory shift — when the ground moves, Strategic Fitness provides a rapid, structured mechanism to re-establish strategic clarity before the next set of decisions are made.

Good fit when

Something significant has changed — or is about to — and the organisation needs to understand what that means for its strategy before acting.
